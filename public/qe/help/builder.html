Questionnaire Designer Help

Pages

Pages are the individual forms which make up a questionnaire. There must be at least one page in your questionnaire, but there is no limit to the number of pages allowed.

To add a page, click the "Add a page" link at the bottom of the list of pages.

To edit a page's title, click the text of the title in the preview area on the right.

To edit the name of the questionnaire, click the text of the questionnaire's title in the preview area on the right.

Elements

Elements are the individual questions on a page. The list of elements can be seen by clicking on the "Elements" tab. To add an element, simply click the type you wish to add to your form. Once an element has been added to a page, you can edit, reorder and add elements to grids.

To edit an element, click on the 'pencil' icon [ ].

To reorder elements, click on the 'drag' icon [ ] and drag the element to the desired position in the list. If you need to scroll to get to the desired position, you can use either the scroll wheel on your mouse or your keyboard (arrows, page up/down, etc).

To add an element to a grid, click on the 'drag' icon [ ] and drag the element onto the grid, within the gray border of the grid.

To remove an element from a grid, click the 'remove' icon [ ].
